1. About the App

Uply: Wash to Wake is an iOS alarm application designed to help users wake up by requiring completion of physical wake-up missions before the alarm can be dismissed. The App is developed and published by Abdul Rehman.

Current wake-up missions include:

  • Wash to Wake: The user must wash their face with water and present their wet face to the camera. The App uses on-device image analysis to detect visual moisture cues.
  • Pushups: The user must complete a set number of pushups. The App uses on-device body-pose tracking to verify rep completion.
