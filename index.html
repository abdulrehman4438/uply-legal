Wash to Wake Mission

Streaks & Sleep Insights

Smart Wet-Face Scan

Uply uses advanced on-device computer vision models to evaluate face wetness. Just wash your face, present it to the camera, and the alarm dismisses instantly.

Anti-Snooze Shield

Uply can optionally restrict access to distracting apps and prevent app deletion during an active alarm, ensuring you get out of bed.

100% On-Device Privacy

Your privacy is absolute. All video frames, pose coordinates, and scanning calculations happen locally in temporary memory. No images or media ever leave your device.

iOS Native Optimization

Designed natively for iOS using SwiftUI and SwiftData. Includes local notifications that work offline and Lock Screen widgets for quick access to your routine.
