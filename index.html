Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 11, 2026

1. Overview and Core Philosophy

At Uply, we believe that your morning routine, your biometric markers, and your personal environment are strictly private. We do not sell, rent, or monetize your personal data. The core functionality of Uply (Wash to Wake alarm verification and Pushups detection) operates entirely on-device.

2. On-Device Camera Processing

Uply uses your iOS device's front-facing camera to verify your wake-up missions:

  • Wash to Wake:
  • Pushups: The camera utilizes on-device pose estimation (via Apple's Vision framework) to count repetitions.

Crucially, no video feed, photos, or raw camera data are ever saved, stored, or transmitted over the network. The camera stream is processed in temporary memory buffers and immediately discarded once the frame is analyzed. The app works 100% offline.

3. Local Persistence

Your alarm schedules, streak history, achievements, and settings are saved locally on your device using Apple's local storage (UserDefaults and SwiftData). This data remains on your physical device unless you delete the app.

4. Permissions and System Access

Uply requests the following system permissions, which you can manage at any time in iOS Settings:

  • Camera: Required to analyze moisture and detect pushups during alarms. The app cannot function without this permission.
  • Notifications: Required to trigger local alarms and send reminders.

5. Compliance with Apple Guidelines

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines (Section 5.1 - Privacy). We do not collect biometric data as defined by iOS security standards; the face moisture analysis is a high-level visual heuristic and does not perform facial recognition, mapping, or user identification.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy practices, please contact us at support@uplyapp.com.
